Comments on a different essay (financial maths)



Understanding 8/50
Content 15/30
Presentation 7/10
References 7/10

Total 37/100

Comments:

"Why use automated trading?"

What is the sense of 'why' in this title? Is the assumption that 'people' should use automatic trading? Should all traders use automatic trading? Is the author saying that the author wants them to use it? Or they want to use it, or they do use it, or it is best if they use it more, best for whom?

"Another important reason..."

It is important for trading decisions to happen quickly? What is the importance of it, is it important for traders to have more profit, does this mean all traders should have more profit? Explain it a bit?

"..upward momentum and vice versa."


The definition of 'momentum' keeps changing in this part of the essay.

"often less reliable"

What is the meaning of 'reliable' here?

"an indicator of the future value of the market"

complete nonsense.


"this indicates that while the Euro might have appreciated against the dollar very recently its long term trend is probably downward."

Trend is not defined and the sentence is nonsense.


"gives more influence to the latest prices."

Nonsense. If we reward this essay we'll be giving influence to a particular theory of prediction but that is the only sensible meaning of 'influence' here

"indicates that the price is likely to be turning downwards"

Nonsense

"under pressure"
Nonsense

Bollinger bands

There is some slight sense here. Variations from day to day were once experimentally found to be Levi-stably distributed (not normally), but standard deviation still has a meaning. But a sense that graphing the standard deviation gives a 'precise idea of relatively high and low prices' is nonsense. I think that Bollinger bands are something like standard deviation during the last fortnight, week or month (forget which).

"A high %B could therefore be representative of a strong rising market"

Nonsense and representative is not defined.

"Fibonacci Retracements"

Nonsense.

"The tenkan crossing the Kijun from below is bullish"

Nonsense.


"S(T) is geometric Brownian motion"

Defining one term with another.


"So here we see by inspection that ...Brownian motion is adapted"

I don't see it from the essay.


"...longer hours of trading than would be possible...."


"this analysis is a self-fulfilling prophesy"

Interesting idea.


My Overall Comments:

This essay fails to consider trading strategies which people use in practise such as arbitrage or evaluating commodities or derivatives. It seems to assume that financial analysis consists of extrapolating a function of one real variable using fibonacci numbers or similar tricks.

More importantly, the essay fails to relate the things it does discuss to concepts such as Mathematics, logic, statistics of more than one variable, philosophy, history, government, regulation, economics, intentionality, society, etc.
