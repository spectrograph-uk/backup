 Erickson 1977      The data refer to the isotope 4He. The energy level values were derived from Erickson's theoretical values of the total energies by scaling them to the current values of the fundamental constants, as given by Mohr et al. 2008 , and by applying an additional correction to the nS levels, as described by Kramida et al. 2008 . The ionization limit was obtained from the theoretical value given by Johnson and Soff 1985 by scaling it to the current values of the fundamental constants. Its uncertainty is estimated to be ±0.0002 cm-1. Uncertainties of differences of all energy level values from the ionization limit are estimated to be within 2 to 20 in the units of the last given decimal place. Literature on He II Energy Levels
