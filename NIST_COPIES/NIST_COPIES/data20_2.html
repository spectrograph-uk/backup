 Sugar and Corliss 1985 , Liang et al. 2005 , West et al. 2001      Most of the level values are quoted from the compilation of Sugar and Corliss 1985 . They were originally determined by Borgström 1968 , Borgström 1971 , and by Hansen et al. 1975 . The uncertainty of excited levels relative to the ground level is estimated to be ±1.3 cm-1. Uncertainties of separations between excited levels are best expressed relative to the 3s23p54p 3P1 level, which has the greatest number of accurately measured observed transitions.These relative uncertainties vary between 2 and 25 units of the last decimal place. The 3s3p64p and 5p 1P°1 levels found by Kastner et al. 1977 are estimated to have uncertainties of 40 cm-1 and 50 cm-1, respectively. Higher members of the same series with principal quantum numbers n = 6–9, found by West et al. 2001 , have uncertainties in the range 160–300 cm-1. Several tens of highly excited 3s23p5ns,nd levels with J = 1 were found by van Kampen et al. 1995 . Their energies were semiempirically fitted by Liang et al. 2005 using the multichannel quantum defect theory. Uncertainties of these fitted energies are expected to be smaller than 50 cm-1. The present data set includes only levels with n ≤ 11 from Liang et al. 2005 .

   Percentage compositions of the wavefunctions were determined by A. Kramida (unpublished, 2013) from parametric fitting of all known energy levels using Cowan's codes . For even parity, 64 known levels were fitted with 19 independent parameters with a standard deviation of 62 cm-1. For odd parity, 121 levels were fitted with 33 independent parameters with a standard deviation of 95 cm-1. For the 3s23p53d, 4s, 4p, and 3s3p6nl configurations, the purest wavefunctions were obtained in the LS coupling scheme, while the rest of the levels are much better described by the jK coupling scheme. The jJ coupling gives slightly lower purity of wavefunctions for some configurations, so the jK coupling was preferred in the present data set. This data set was prepared by A. Kramida in March 2013.
